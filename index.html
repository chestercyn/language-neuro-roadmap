Ultimate goal

Five axioms

    How the pillars are derived

    From axiom 1 (language = networks) → Pillar 1 (foundations) and Pillar 3 (advanced connectomics) are mandatory. From axiom 2 (disease = perturbation) and axiom 3 (stimulation = reshaping) → Pillar 4 (causal neuromodulation) bridges mechanism to clinic. From axiom 1's spatial-temporal demands → Pillar 2 (multimodal imaging) widens the lens beyond EEG. From axiom 4 (rigour) and axiom 5 (cross-disciplinary leverage) → Pillar 5 (clinical translation, ML, lab leadership) is the synthesis layer where you become more than the sum of your methods.
